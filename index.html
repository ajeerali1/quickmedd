About the App

Our Hospital Queue Management App is designed to simplify and streamline the appointment process for patients. Instead of standing in long queues at hospitals, users can easily book their appointments or get a token online through the app.

The app aims to reduce waiting time, manage crowd flow, and improve overall patient experience by offering a simple, accessible solution right from their mobile devices or computers.

Key features include:

  • Online appointment booking and token system
  • Real-time updates on queue status
  • User-friendly interface for all age groups
  • Designed prototype in Figma
  • Storyboard and video pitch for better understanding

This app was created with a mission to make healthcare access more efficient, especially in busy hospitals where time is critical.
